Key Features

Driver Drowsiness Detection

Detects signs of driver drowsiness using AI-driven analysis of eye movements.

Alerts the driver if drowsiness is detected to prevent accidents.

Distraction Monitoring (Phone Use)

Monitors if the driver is distracted by their phone or other devices.

Issues alerts when dangerous distractions are detected while driving.

Distraction Monitoring (Looking Away)

Tracks the driver's eye movements to ensure they're focused on the road.

Alerts the driver if they look away from the road for too long.

Eye Blink Sensor for Speed Control

Monitors blink patterns to assess driver fatigue or inattention.

Automatically adjusts vehicle speed if dangerous drowsiness is detected.

Real-time Alerts & Reports

Instantly notifies the driver of any detected issues, like drowsiness or distractions.

Provides detailed reports for further analysis and improvement of driver safety.

How It Works

Our AI model tracks eye movements to detect drowsiness or distraction. If the driver closes their eyes for 3-4 seconds, an alert is triggered. If closed for 5 seconds, the system automatically slows down the vehicle while sounding an alert.
